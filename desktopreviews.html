Desktop Reviews

Discover reviews for the top selling desktops of 2023

It is important to always do research whenever doing a large purchase. You always want to make sure you get what you pay for and know what you are getting yourself into. Here are the top rated desktops of 2023!

Dell XPS Desktop

The Dell XPS Desktop is a powerful and sleek computer designed for high performance and productivity. It features 11th Gen Intel Core processors and up to 64GB of DDR4 RAM, providing plenty of speed and power to handle demanding tasks such as video editing, gaming, and 3D modeling. The desktop also comes with a range of storage options including SSDs and HDDs, allowing for plenty of space to store files and applications. The XPS Desktop has a compact design with a modern and minimalist aesthetic that will look great in any workspace. It also features a variety of ports including USB-C, HDMI, and DisplayPort, making it easy to connect to peripherals and external displays. Overall, the Dell XPS Desktop is a versatile and reliable computer that's perfect for professionals and power users who require top-tier performance and flexibility. Starting Price at $749

Apple Mac Mini 2023

The Apple Mac Mini 2023 is the latest iteration of Apple's compact and powerful desktop computer. It comes equipped with the latest M-series chip, the M2, which is specifically designed for the Mac platform and promises even better performance and energy efficiency than its predecessor. The Mac Mini 2023 also features up to 16GB of unified memory and up to 2TB of SSD storage, making it perfect for demanding tasks such as video editing and graphic design. In addition to its impressive hardware, the Mac Mini 2023 also features a range of ports including USB-C, Thunderbolt, HDMI, and Ethernet, providing excellent connectivity options for peripherals and external displays. The sleek and compact design of the Mac Mini 2023 makes it a great fit for any workspace, whether it's in the office or at home. Overall, the Apple Mac Mini 2023 is a powerful and versatile desktop computer that's perfect for anyone who needs top-tier performance in a small form factor. Starting Price at $599

Corsair One i300

The Corsair One i300 is a small form factor gaming PC designed for enthusiasts who demand high performance in a compact package. It is powered by a 10th Gen Intel Core i7 processor and an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080 graphics card, providing incredible gaming performance and rendering capabilities. The Corsair One i300 also features 32GB of DDR4 RAM and a 1TB NVMe SSD, providing ample storage and quick access to files and applications. Despite its small form factor, the Corsair One i300 comes equipped with a range of ports including USB 3.2, HDMI, DisplayPort, and Ethernet, allowing for easy connectivity to peripherals and external displays. The compact design of the Corsair One i300 makes it easy to transport to LAN parties or other gaming events, while its powerful components make it perfect for demanding games and applications. Overall, the Corsair One i300 is a powerful and portable gaming PC that's perfect for enthusiasts who demand top-tier performance in a compact form factor. Starting Price at $3999
