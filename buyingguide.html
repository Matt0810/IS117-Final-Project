Buying Guide

Not sure why you need a computer for college? You've come to the right place!

As a new college student, choosing the right computer is an important decision that can have a big impact on your academic success. Whether you prefer the portability of a laptop or the power of a desktop, it's essential to consider a variety of factors before making a purchase. In this guide, we'll help you navigate the buying process by breaking down the most important features to consider for both laptops and desktops.

Factors to consider when buying a laptop

  • Portability
  • Battery Life
  • Display
  • Processing Power
  • Memory and Storage

Factors to consider when buying a desktop

  • Processing Power
  • Memory and Storage
  • Display
  • Graphics
  • Upgradability
