Laptop Reviews

Discover reviews for the top selling laptops of 2023

It is important to always do research whenever doing a large purchase. You always want to make sure you get what you pay for and know what you are getting yourself into. Here are the top rated laptops of 2023!

Macbook Pro M2

Apple's latest installment of the Macbook Pro includes their new powerful M2 chip. The new M2 chips is perfect for individuals focused on video editing since it has the fastest video transcoding than any other Apple laptop. The display is a Liquid Retina XDR which makes colors look true and creates a display that is amazing to look at. The Macbook Pro M2 also has the longest battery life ever in a Mac, 18 hours of video playback and 12 hours of wireless web browsing. Starting Price at $1999

Dell XPS 13

The Dell XPS 13 is a popular high-end laptop that is designed for both personal and professional use. It features a sleek, lightweight design and a durable build quality, making it easy to carry around and work on-the-go. The laptop has a 13.3-inch InfinityEdge display that is available in both Full HD and 4K resolution options, providing a clear and vivid visual experience. The XPS 13 is powered by Intel's latest processors and offers a range of storage and memory options to choose from. It also includes a variety of ports, including Thunderbolt 4 and USB-C, which allow for fast data transfer and charging. With its impressive performance, high-quality display, and portability, the Dell XPS 13 is a great option for those looking for a top-of-the-line laptop. Starting Price at $849

Microsoft Surface Pro 8

The Microsoft Surface Pro 8 is the latest iteration of Microsoft's popular 2-in-1 tablet/laptop hybrid series. It features a vibrant 13-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 2880x1920 pixels, making it perfect for streaming media and working on creative projects. The device is powered by 11th Gen Intel Core processors and Intel Iris Xe graphics, providing impressive performance and graphics capabilities. The Surface Pro 8 also comes with up to 16GB of RAM and up to 1TB of SSD storage, providing plenty of space to store files and applications. Additionally, it features a versatile kickstand and a detachable keyboard, making it easy to use as a tablet or laptop depending on your needs. The Surface Pro 8 is a powerful and versatile device that's ideal for both personal and professional use. Starting Price at $900
